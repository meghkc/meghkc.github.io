I am a Ph.D. candidate in Civil Engineering, specializing in transportation systems and smart mobility. My research focuses on electric vehicle integration, transit scheduling optimization, and data-driven decision-making for sustainable transportation planning. Through a combination of operations research, Machine/ Deep Reinforcement Learning, and simulation modeling, I aim to develop innovative solutions for the evolving challenges in public transit and urban mobility.

Explore my work to learn more about my academic journey, research projects, and publications.
